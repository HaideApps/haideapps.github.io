Privacy Policy — Mosquito: Pitch Black

Effective date: 19 May 2026. Developer: Ragnar Haide / HaideApps. Contact: appshaide@gmail.com.

Short version

The Mosquito: Pitch Black app does not collect, store, transmit or share any data about you. It has no internet permission, no accounts, no logins, no cloud service. The only data the app touches is its own resource files (the mosquito sound, the cartoon image) packaged inside the APK.

What data the app collects

None.

What permissions the app uses

  • android.permission.VIBRATE — used only to trigger the in-app haptic feedback (the "mosquito is biting you" sustained pulse and the slap feedback). Vibration cannot be used to identify or track a user.

The app does NOT request internet, microphone, camera, contacts, location, storage, or any other permission.

Network access

The app makes no network requests. Because INTERNET is not declared in the manifest, Android prevents network usage at the system level.

Third-party services

None. The app does not include analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, social network SDKs, or any other third-party service.

Children

The app is marked 18+ in Play Console for compliance reasons, but contains no content harmful to children — no violence beyond cartoon bug swatting, no explicit content, no in-app purchases, no advertisements, no chat or user-generated content.

Data deletion

Since the app collects no data, there is nothing to delete. Uninstalling the app removes all on-device files.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new effective date.
